Soda Springs, Idaho

Soda Springs is a community rich in history and overflowing with the potential for growth and expansion. It has the safety, friendliness and support of a small town while also offering a prime location for industry, agriculture and business. Soda Springs features five beautiful parks that offer playgrounds, pavilions, hiking, biking and cross country ski trails surrounded in nature, as well as an 18-hole disc golf course. Within 5 to 20 minutes of Soda Springs is access to swimming, fishing, boating, snow machining, camping, ATV trails, bird watching and hunting. See more...
